Find Your 'Fancy'...

Whether you are newly engaged, about to embark on this next chapter of your love story - or simply dreaming about it (don't we all!) we're excited to help you on your quest to find your perfect ring!

When dreaming up your perfect engagement ring, the first characteristic you should decide is the shape of diamond you prefer.
Diamond shape refers to the shape in which it has been cut into and influences the complete look and feel of a given design. The most popular shape is the round diamond - perhaps due to its classic, timeless appeal - but also because this particular shape tends to generate more sparkle when compared to other shapes.
However, there are so many other interesting shapes to choose from, and we refer to these as 'fancy cut' - you can even opt for a diamond in the shape of a heart! Shapes such as the cushion cut have also surged back into popularity, offering a more vintage alternative with its rounded corners and a soft sparkle - if you're looking for something a bit different. It is definitely worth visiting your local jeweller to try on diamond rings with different shapes, so you can determine which shape is most flattering on your hand and offers the right amount of sparkle for you! üíç
